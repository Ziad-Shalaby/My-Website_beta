About Me

Hi, I’m Ziad!
I’m a 20-year-old Data Science student in my third year at Alexandria University, passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights.
My journey into data science began with a love for mathematics. I’ve always been fascinated by numbers and their ability to tell stories.
Over the past couple of years, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several exciting projects, ranging from predictive analytics to Natural Language Processing (NLP).
One of my favorite projects involved building a recommendation system for local businesses, which helped connect customers with their ideal products.
This experience taught me how impactful data science can be in solving real-world problems.

What I love most about data science is the combination of creativity and logic it requires. Whether it’s cleaning messy datasets, visualizing trends, or training machine learning models, each step feels like solving a challenging puzzle.
I’ve grown comfortable using tools like Python, pandas, scikit-learn, and SQL to work with data, and I’m always eager to learn new techniques and technologies.

Right now, I’m focusing on sharpening my skills in machine learning and diving deeper into solving complex business problems with data-driven approaches.
Beyond the technical side, I enjoy collaborating with others and learning from different perspectives.
I believe data science is as much about people as it is about data—and I’m excited to use my skills to make a difference.
